PETIT SEMINAIRE HR. SEC. SCHOOL

  • 🎓 Overview Founded in 1792 originally as a seminary-college. Its current building dates back to 1844–46 
  • Was upgraded over the years: English-medium section began in 1904; matriculation-level status in 1932 under Madras University; adopted the 10+2 system in 1978 
  • 📚 Academics & Culture Now a CBSE-affiliated higher secondary school with over 7,600 students and 220 staff members 
  • Known for strong emphasis on discipline, character-building, and excellence not just in academics but also sports, co-/extra‑curriculars, and preparing students for professional careers 
  • School motto: “Nil Magnum Nisi Bonum” — “Nothing is great unless it is good” 
  • 🏫 Campus & Facilities A sprawling heritage campus located at Dr. M G R Nagar, Moolakulum, Puducherry – 605010 
  • Offers modern infrastructure, dedicated spaces for sports, a library, science labs, and scholarship programmes
